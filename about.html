Our story

Why we started Trident CloudNet

After more than a decade working inside large enterprises and managed service providers, our founder kept seeing the same problem: small and mid-sized businesses were either paying enterprise prices for support they didn't need, or settling for underpowered IT that couldn't keep up with their growth. And in the networking and software world, product teams were shipping without rigorous independent testing because building an internal QA lab was too expensive.

The answer was a smarter model — one that used AI to eliminate repetitive noise, India-based engineers to provide affordable around-the-clock coverage, and US-based senior engineers to handle the complex problems and senior test architecture that truly require experience and judgment.

That's Trident CloudNet. Not a staffing agency. Not a generic helpdesk vendor. A purpose-built IT operations and product testing company — serving the businesses that need their IT managed, and the engineering teams that need their products tested right before they ship.

How we operate

Three layers that work together

Our US + India + AI model isn't about cutting corners — it's about routing every issue to the right resource at the right cost, every time.

🤖

AI — the first responder

Every ticket is logged, classified, and routed by AI. Known issues are resolved instantly — no human wait time. The AI also suppresses alert noise so engineers only see what matters.

~40% of tickets resolved automatically
🇮🇳

India team — always on

Our India-based L1/L2 team works overnight US hours, providing cost-effective around-the-clock coverage for the issues AI can't resolve. Skilled, experienced, and English-fluent engineers.

Overnight US coverage · ~$30/hr avg cost
🇺🇸

US team — senior expertise

US-based senior engineers handle complex escalations, cloud architecture, major incidents, and strategic advisory. You get enterprise-grade expertise when it truly matters.

CCNP · AWS SA · Azure Admin certified
