How it works

ChatOps captures context.
Engineers resolve the work.

Our managed-services model gives engineers device diagnostics, alert context, and runbooks before they start troubleshooting.

1

Issue comes in

Via email, phone, or your client portal. Logged instantly into our ticketing system with full context captured.

2

ChatOps diagnoses

Recognizes the request, queries registered devices over SSH, and returns structured interface, route, BGP/OSPF, ping, and traceroute results.

3

India engineers resolve

The India L1/L2 team uses ChatOps diagnostics and runbooks to handle incidents, then escalates only client-side vendor, hardware, or policy decisions.

4

You get the summary

Tickets close with clear notes, and health reports call out recurring issues, device risks, and recommended next actions.
