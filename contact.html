FAQ

Common questions

Is the free audit really free? What's the catch? +
Genuinely free. 30 minutes with a senior engineer who will look at your environment and give you honest feedback. We do this because we believe the best way to earn your business is to show you what we can do — not sell you on it.
How quickly can you onboard us if we decide to move forward? +
For Starter and Growth plans, we can typically onboard within 5–7 business days. Enterprise onboarding takes 2–3 weeks due to custom SLA configuration and dedicated account setup. We can often provide immediate coverage for urgent situations while onboarding continues.
Do I need to give you access to my systems before the audit? +
Not for the initial call. The audit is a conversation — we'll ask questions about your setup, current pain points, and goals. If you want us to do a deeper technical review, we can arrange read-only access after signing an NDA.
We already have an internal IT person. Can you work alongside them? +
Absolutely. Many of our clients have an internal IT person or small team. We complement them — handling the after-hours coverage, L3 escalations, cloud ops, and monitoring that would otherwise require a much larger internal headcount.
What industries do you work with? +
We work across industries — manufacturing, professional services, healthcare, fintech, retail, and more. Our focus is on the size of the challenge, not the industry vertical. If you run business-critical infrastructure and need reliable support, we can help.
