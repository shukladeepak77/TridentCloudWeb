FAQ

Pricing questions, answered

Everything you need to know before signing up.

Is there a setup fee or long-term contract? +
No setup fee and no long-term contract. All plans are billed month-to-month. You can cancel or change plans with 30 days' notice. We earn your business every month.
What happens if I go over my user seat count? +
We'll reach out to discuss upgrading your plan before it becomes a problem. We won't shut you off — we'll work with you to find the right fit. Enterprise plans have no seat limit.
Can I mix US and India engineers? +
Yes. Our Growth and Enterprise plans include both teams by default. You benefit from India's cost-effective overnight coverage and US senior engineers during business hours — all under one SLA.
How does the free network audit work? +
It's a 30-minute call with a senior engineer. We review your current setup, identify gaps, and give you an honest assessment of what's working and what isn't. No obligation. We won't pressure you into a plan.
Can I add staff augmentation hours to any plan? +
Yes. Staff augmentation is available as an add-on to any plan at the hourly rates listed above. Enterprise plans include 4 complimentary hours per month. For larger needs, we can structure a retainer.
Do you offer discounts for annual billing? +
Yes — we offer a 10% discount for annual pre-payment. Contact us to set up annual billing on any plan.
What ticketing system do you use? Can it integrate with ours? +
We use our own client portal by default, but we integrate with ServiceNow, Jira Service Desk, Freshdesk, and Zendesk. We can also work within your existing system if preferred.
Is my data and environment information kept confidential? +
Absolutely. We sign a mutual NDA before onboarding. All engineers operate under strict confidentiality agreements, and we follow SOC 2-aligned data handling practices. We never share client information with third parties.
