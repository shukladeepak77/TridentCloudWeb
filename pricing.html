FAQ

Pricing questions, answered

Everything you need to know before requesting a ChatOps quote.

Why is pricing custom? +
ChatOps pricing depends on the number of devices, network vendors, deployment model, integrations, and custom workflow requirements. We scope the environment first so the quote is realistic.
Is this a managed support plan? +
No. This pricing page is for the Trident ChatOps product. The product helps teams query devices, capture diagnostics, review alerts, follow runbooks, and maintain an audit trail.
Does ChatOps resolve tickets automatically? +
No. ChatOps provides diagnostics, evidence, runbook context, and workflow support. Human operators remain responsible for approvals, ticket decisions, and production changes.
Can it run inside our environment? +
Yes. ChatOps is designed for on-premise or customer-controlled VM deployment, with inventory and credentials kept inside the client environment.
Which network vendors can it support? +
The product can be scoped for common network platforms such as Cisco, Arista, Juniper, and Linux-based systems. Vendor coverage is confirmed during discovery.
Can we start with a small pilot? +
Yes. A pilot can focus on a limited device set and a small group of diagnostic workflows before expanding into broader operations coverage.
